Hummus for the falafel

Description

I mean, it is basically the same as falafel, only moist.

Let's be honest, falafel is divine, but reaaally dry. Solution: Hummus!

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • More chickpeas
  • Don't forget the chickpeas
  • Tahini
  • Lemon
  • Water
  • Maybe some olive oil, but not too much

Steps

  1. Don't cook the chickpeas (buy the canned onnes, they are cooked!)
  2. Munsh it all together (use your fingers if you're strong, use a blender if not.)
  3. Enjoy the dive.
